Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface wich let you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos and Markdown. Extensiblity with plugins and themes provide easy ways to add functionability or change the looks of a blog.

Robust content management

Flexible conent management enables users to easily move trough posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionability, you are in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speed in mind, worldwide data centers provide the backbone of ultra-fast connectivity. This ensure your site will load instantly, no matter where your readers are, keeping your site competitive.

Free, Open, Simple

Blogr is free and open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools and work seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.
